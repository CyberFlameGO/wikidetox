Overview

We are interested in understanding the dynamics of online conversation which might lead to toxicity or conflicts. Please use the following definition of toxicity as a guide:

  • A toxic comment is a rude, disrespectful, or unreasonable comment that is somewhat likely to make you leave a discussion or give up on sharing your perspective.

In this exercise, please read the conversation provided and answer the questions about the conversation and the final comment (highlighted in blue).

We Provide

  • A section title about the conversation
  • A partial conversation as it occurred on Wikipedia
  • A final comment in the conversation highlighted in blue
  • A set of questions about toxicity, disagreement, and passive aggressiveness contained in the conversation

Process

  1. Read the comment
  2. If the conversation is not in English, please indicate that on the checkbox. 
  3. Answer the questions regarding the conversation and the final comment.
