Overview

We are interested in understanding when online conversation leads to toxic interactions. Please use the following definition of toxicity as a guide:

  • A toxic comment is a rude, disrespectful, or unreasonable comment that is somewhat likely to make you leave a discussion or give up on sharing your perspective.

We Provide

  • A page title the conversation is happening within
  • A section title about the conversation
  • A thread of conversation as it occurred on Wikipedia

Please read the conversation and answer the questions.

  1. Read each comment
  2. If the conversation is not written in English, please select the checkbox noting that the conversation is not in English, and skip the other questions.
  3. Answer the all the questions; they ask about the conversation and the final comment.
