Overview

We are interested in identifying toxic comments in online discussions. Please use the following definition of toxicity as a guide:

  • A toxic comment is a rude, disrespectful, or unreasonable comment that is somewhat likely to make someone leave a discussion, engage in fights, or give up on sharing their perspective.
  • Toxicity should not be confused with civil disagreement.
You will read conversations that took place among Wikipedia editors. For each conversation, we show:
  • The title of the Wikipedia page where the conversation took place;
  • The section title of the conversation (in the format ==section title==);
  • The comments making up the conversation.
