Gangaram pala was born on 16 April 1996 in Mangalpur in a Brahmin family from Biswanath pala, former Marketing Peon The Aska Sugar Factory,  Ganga was educated at the prestigious Govt. Upper Primary School in Mangalpur, and later the Govt. High School,Mangalpur. After that he attended NBC College, Berhampur University, and he qualified for Bachelor of Arts degree.

Birth and childhood[ସମ୍ପାଦନା]
Ganga was born Gangaram pala (shortened to Ganga ) at his ancestral home at Near Radha govinda Temple, Street in Mangalpur,Aska the capital of Bhubaneswar India, on 16 April 1996 during the masik sivaratri festival. He belonged to a traditional brahmin,Halua family. His father, Vishwanath Pala, was an Marketing Peon at the Aska Sugar Factroy. Govinda Pala, Ganga's grandfather was a Sanskrit and Persian scholar who left his family and became a monk at age twenty-five. His mother, Bishnu Devi, was a devout housewife. The progressive, rational attitude of Ganga's father and the religious temperament of his mother helped shape his thinking and personality.
Gangaram was interested spiritually from a young age, and used to meditate before the images of deities such as Shiva, Rama, Satyasai, and Mahavir Hanuman. He was fascinated by wandering ascetics and monks. Ganga was naughty and restless as a child, and his parents often had difficulty controlling him.
Education[ସମ୍ପାଦନା]
In 2001, at the age of Five ,Ganga enrolled at Govt, Upper Primary School,Mangalpur,Aska, where he went to school until his family moved to Aska in 2001. In 2003, after his family's return to Mangalpur, he was the only student to receive first-division marks in the NBC College entrance examination.  He was an avid reader in a wide range of subjects, including Economics, Political Science,Social Science and art and literature. He was also interested in Hindu scriptures, including the Vedas, the Upanishads, the Bhagavad Gita, the Ramayana, the Mahabharata and the Puranas. Ganga was trained in Indian classical music, and regularly participated in physical exercise, sports and organised activities.  In 2016 he passed the Final Arts examination, and completed a Bachelor of Arts degree at NBC College,Kendupadar,Ganjam.
